# Role profile: Product Manager (technical / AI-PM)

For PM roles at top-tier product / AI companies, with a technical leaning.

## priority_keywords

product strategy, roadmap, prioritization, RICE, MoSCoW, OKRs, KPIs,
north-star metric, lifecycle, GTM, launch, beta, rollout, A/B testing,
experimentation, customer interviews, user research, usability testing,
NPS, CSAT, retention, activation, engagement, conversion, funnel, cohort,
TAM, SAM, SOM, competitive analysis, pricing, packaging, segmentation,
SQL, Tableau, Looker, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Heap, Pendo, FullStory,
Figma, prototyping, wireframe, spec writing, PRD, RFC, design review,
cross-functional, stakeholder management, executive communication,
roadmap presentation, JIRA, Confluence, Notion, Linear, agile, scrum.

## hiring_signals

- A launched feature with quantified user-facing impact.
- A killed product / killed feature decision with rationale.
- A pricing or packaging change owned end-to-end.
- A customer-discovery insight that altered the roadmap.
- A cross-functional partnership (eng / design / DS / GTM) named.

## red_flags

- All bullets are "managed roadmap" / "drove alignment" with no metric.
- No customer-facing metric (only internal "delivered on time").
- No mention of an experiment or its result.
- Marketing-flavoured bullets ("evangelised the product") on a PM resume.

## seniority_calibration

- Senior PM: owned a product area, shipped 2-3 launches with metrics.
- Group PM / Lead: cross-pillar strategy, manager of PMs.
- Director / VP: P&L, org-level strategy, executive presence.
