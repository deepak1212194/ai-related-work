> Role-specific emphasis loaded by the agent when the user selects this role.

## Target Role

Product Manager (4+ years)

## What to Emphasize

- Launches with quantified outcomes (DAU growth, retention, NPS, revenue)
- Cross-functional leadership (engineering, design, data)
- Product decisions backed by data and user research
