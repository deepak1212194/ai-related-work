# Skill: Data Scientist Role

> Role-specific emphasis loaded by the agent when the user selects this role.

## Target Role

Data Scientist (4+ years)

## What to Emphasize

- Business impact: revenue lift, conversion, churn reduction, cost savings
- Hypothesis-driven approach and experimental design
- Statistical rigor: A/B testing, causal inference, confidence intervals
- Modeling techniques and feature engineering
- Communication: presenting to stakeholders, translating business to tech

## Priority Keywords

SQL, Python, pandas, scikit-learn, A/B testing, causal inference,
statistical modeling, Tableau, Looker, BigQuery, Snowflake,
experimentation, feature engineering, XGBoost, LightGBM, time-series
