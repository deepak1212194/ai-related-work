# Role profile: Data Scientist

For applied data scientist / research engineer / analytics ML roles at
product companies, FAANG-tier data orgs, and growth / experimentation teams.

## priority_keywords

experimentation, A/B testing, causal inference, regression discontinuity,
diff-in-diff, propensity score, uplift modeling, lift, holdout, treatment,
control, statistical significance, p-value, confidence interval, hypothesis,
metric design, KPI, north star, churn, retention, LTV, conversion, funnel,
cohort, segmentation, forecasting, time-series, Prophet, ARIMA, SARIMAX,
gradient boosting, XGBoost, LightGBM, scikit-learn, Python, R, SQL,
notebooks, Jupyter, dbt, Airflow, Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, Tableau,
Looker, dashboarding, stakeholder communication.

## hiring_signals

- A business question the candidate translated into a measurable outcome.
- A counterfactual / experimental design they ran or analysed.
- A metric definition they authored that became a team standard.
- A causal inference technique applied to an observational dataset.
- An ML model whose deployment was justified by ROI, not novelty.

## red_flags

- All projects are Kaggle-style accuracy chases with no business linkage.
- "Deployed model" without any metric on real users.
- Heavy DL stack with no statistical rigour discussion.
- Using "AI" as a synonym for "ML" without naming the algorithm.

## seniority_calibration

- L4-L5 (senior): expects 1-2 launched experiments with metric impact.
- L6 (staff): expects experimentation framework / metric design ownership.
- L7+ (principal): expects strategy-level guidance, multi-team influence.
